A Place to Call HOME

You can never really know someone without visiting their home. We found some brave people, who live in alternative homes, and agreed to open their door to us, and to you. Press start, pick a home, and start scrolling.

Tip: feel free to stop at the rooms and scan with your mouse, you will discover something about the people who lives there.

About the project

My name is Oral Shmueli, I am a Digital Media student from
Tel Aviv University and the creator of â€œA Place to Call Home. Since I can remember, I always dreamed to design my own home. From a small age, I dreamed of a huge window in the living room which I can see the sunset from.

It was clear to me that my study project would also be related to this dream, which over the years became more and more dominant in my life. Amazingly, during the project I bought my own house, with a huge living room window, which faces west of course.

In part of the project, I also conducted research on the human need for a home, which confronted me with my own dream, and made me rethink the meaning of the home in our lives and its psychological functions. In addition to the essential territorial need of a home, the idea of home also fulfills critical mental needs. Home is a reflection of our soul, is an essential part of our self-determination. We organize it, design it, and fill it with items that will remind us repeatedly who we are. I invite you to explore yourself through my project, to look around you, at your own home, and try to figure out who you are through it.
